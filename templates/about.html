About us

    Wavelength is a startup in UC Davis' PLASMA accelerator program. We're a team of 4 UC Davis students: Sean (founder, 4th year), Ciel (co-founder, 4th year), Nathan (tech lead, 2nd year), and Rishit (tech lead, 2nd year).

    The idea started as two passing thoughts: it would be cool to know who around us has our same music taste, and and that there's something wrong with the endless cycle of deleting and redownloading dating apps (this isn't just us, over 60% of college students who use dating apps have experienced the same!). So, we thought up Wavelength, the dating app that matches you through music and gets you meeting in person through personalized first date suggestions, because sometimes it's just easier to have the date planned out for you.

    We are currently in the process of hosting our first proof-of-concept event, Mix & Match by Wavelength, which is being held in a new Davis art venue, the Secret Spot, and will feature music, food, drinks, and most importantly, people.
